Ars Fauna https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/ars-fauna
- This mod adds new variants of the Starbuncle, Drygmy, and Whirlisprig from Ars Nouveau to the world for ambience.

Mahou Tsukai https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/mahou-tsukai
- Mahou Tsukai is a magic mod focused mainly on spell uniqueness and special effects, instead of focusing on how spells are created, as many magic mods do.

Chest With Legs https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/chest-with-legs
- A mod that adds a new way to store your items. 

Bigger Reactors https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/biggerreactors
- You want power? We got power. Bigger Reactors is an energy generation mod based on the original by Erogenous Beef.

Ars Artifice https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/ars-artifice
- Ars Artifice focuses primarily on a new Artificing mechanic, allowing players to create curios that automatically cast spells when certain events effect the player.

Gamma Creatures https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/gamma-creatures
- Tamable and rideable creatures, new saddles for each creature, new blocks, ores, biomes, and items

CERBON's Better Beacons https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/cerbons-better-beacons/
- An improved version of Minecraft's beacon.

Cognition https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/experience-obelisk/
- Cognition aims to overhaul player XP, by providing new ways to store, transport, and use it in weird and wonderful ways.

Hole Filler Mod https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/hole-filler-mod/
- A lightweight mod consisting of a hole filler. Throw it and walk away. Your hole will be filled in.

Corail Deconstruction Table https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/corail-deconstruction-table/
